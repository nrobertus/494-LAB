Our Mission

Here at TaCo, we strive to creat the best dang tacos known to man. We realize this is a lofty goal, but we believe that if you shoot for the stars, you might just end up creating tacos instead. Some might even call them "stellar tacos."

In addition to tacos, we are aiming to take the grand prize for the worlds spiciest salsa, as well as the world's meatiest burrito. Seriously, our salsa could probably put you in a coma, and the burritos are approaching the density of a dying star. As you can imagine, our worker's compensation insuance is through the roof.
